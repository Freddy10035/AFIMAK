Pricing FAQs

All our prices include an award, which goes directly to the winning creative and crowdspring's fee. When you post your project, you'll choose between 4 different packages: Silver, Gold, Elite and Platinum, each of which includes the award, our fee, and a set of features. You'll always know exactly what you'll pay. There are no hidden costs and no surprises.
We are proud to offer flexible pricing that's perfect for businesses of any size. Silver projects are great for small companies that don't require privacy or collaboration (some of these are available as a la carte options in Silver projects). Gold projects are perfect for slightly more established companies that need additional privacy and control. Elite projects are perfect for companies of any size who want to work with a small group of our very best designers. Platinum projects are great for companies that need maximum privacy and control over their projects. When you post a Platinum project, you'll work solely with our best creatives. These projects include ALL features and are perfect for agencies, funded startups, and established companies.
Multiple awards significantly boost your project's participation because creatives have more chances to win an award. By purchasing multiple entries, you can also combine the different elements or ideas. You can add additional awards when you post your project or after your project posts. You can add as many additional awards as you want – our only rule after a project has started is that the awards must be at least equal to your lowest award.
When you receive your final deliverables in the project and approve them, crowdspring pays the winning creative. At that time, you become the full owner of the intellectual property you purchased in your project.
If you're not completely happy with your design or writing project, or if your naming project doesn't receive at least 100 entries, we'll promptly refund all your money. We're not happy until you're happy. Our guarantee shows that we stand behind our promise to make you happy.
With over 9 years of experience and over 60,000 business clients around the world who love us, crowdspring is the trusted leader when it comes to design and naming. Our flexible pricing is perfect for businesses of any size. If you want lots of choice and affordable, high-quality design and naming, you've come to the right place.
